Donate and Smile

What is AmazonSmile?

AmazonSmile is a website operated by Amazon with the same products, prices, and shopping features as Amazon.com. The difference is that when you shop on AmazonSmile, the AmazonSmile Foundation will donate 0.5% of the purchase price of eligible products to the charitable organization of your choice.

What is Donate and Smile?

In order for Amazon to donate to your charity, you have to shop through the AmazonSmile website. Donate and Smile is a browser extension that automatically redirects you to AmazonSmile, so you don't have to remember to go to the right website.

How do I use Donate and Smile?

Now that you have installed the extension, you just need to make sure that you have selected your charity on Amazon. The redirect will occur as long as you are signed in on Amazon.

How can I check my AmazonSmile impact?

Go to your AmazonSmile dashboard.

Can I temporarily turn off Donate and Smile?

Use the checkbox on the preferences page or in the popup that you can open with the toolbar button. The button will be grey if the redirect is off and orange if it is on.

What if I have an issue or suggestion?

Who made Donate and Smile?

The Sublime Fund team. We are working on getting more people involved in philanthropy, and using AmazonSmile is an easy step that anyone who shops on Amazon should take.

Can I see the code for Donate and Smile?

Donate and Smile is open source, and the source code is available on GitHub.
